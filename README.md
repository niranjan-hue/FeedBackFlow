<h2>🚀 About the Project</h2>

<p>
<strong>FeedBackFlow</strong> is an anonymous feedback platform that allows users to share
honest opinions, suggestions, and concerns without revealing their identity.
</p>

<p>
Many people hesitate to provide genuine feedback when their identity is known.
FeedBackFlow creates a safe environment where users can communicate openly,
helping individuals, teams, and organizations collect valuable insights.
</p>

<p>
The platform focuses on privacy, simplicity, and accessibility while making
feedback collection seamless and effective.
</p>
