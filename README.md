<h2>🚀 About the Project</h2>

<p>
<strong>FeedBackFlow</strong> is an anonymous feedback platform that enables users to share honest opinions, suggestions, and concerns without revealing their identity.
</p>

<p>
The platform helps individuals, communities, teams, and organizations collect meaningful feedback while ensuring complete privacy for users.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>✨ Features</h2>

<ul>
  <li>🔒 Anonymous feedback submission</li>
  <li>📝 Simple and intuitive interface</li>
  <li>📊 Feedback analytics dashboard</li>
  <li>📈 Visual insights and reporting</li>
  <li>🔍 Search and filter feedback</li>
  <li>⚡ Real-time Firebase integration</li>
  <li>📱 Fully responsive design</li>
  <li>🎨 Modern UI with Tailwind CSS</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>🎯 Mission</h2>

<p>
Create a safe space where people can share genuine feedback without fear of judgment, helping organizations and communities grow through honest communication.
</p>
